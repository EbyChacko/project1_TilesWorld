Few Words About Us

TILE'S WORLD is a traditional irish company driven by creativity and an endless passion for helping you find the perfect tile for your project. Each person on our team is eager to develop a long lasting relationship with you.

We are a strong, personality-driven brand that’s carved a unique niche in the renovation and interior design landscape, with a customer experience that remains unmatched in style, service, and selection.

Our goal is to deliver you endless inspiration and the perfect tile pairing for your design project. We can’t wait to work together and make you a part of our family today.

Two brothers from India set off on their voyage with the intention of exceeding their aims. From that point on, Tile's World grew to become one of Ireland’s most prosperous commercial conglomerates with a stronghold in several sectors, including producing plywood, tiles, marketing, and transportation.

For all of your home construction and remodeling requirements, turn to us. Because of our company’s expertise in the distribution of premium plywood and tiles, we can offer our clients a huge range of styles and designs. Because we are dedicated to providing the highest quality items, we only purchase from the most reliable vendors, guaranteeing that our clients will receive the greatest supplies for their projects.

Our Specialities

Quality

At our tile store, we take quality very seriously. Before selling any tile, we ensure that it has gone through a rigorous quality check process. Our team of skilled professionals inspects the tiles to ensure that they meet specific standards and perform as they should over time. We take pride in providing our customers with high-quality tiles that are long-lasting, beautiful, and functional.

Service

Our tile fixing and service team is dedicated to providing high-quality tile installation and repair services. They have the knowledge and expertise to get the job done right the first time, whether you're considering installing new tile floors or repairing existing ones. Our team is skilled in installing and repairing various types of tiles, including ceramic, porcelain, slate, and marble.

Friendly team

The team members in tile's world are knowledgeable about the products and can provide expert advice on design and installation. They are also patient and take the time to understand the customer's needs and preferences. With a wide selection of affordable flooring and tile products, customers can find the perfect tile for their home renovation project. The team members at Tile's World are committed to providing excellent customer service and ensuring that every customer leaves the showroom satisfied with their purchase.
